About Happy Animals

Happy Animals is a unique NFT project dedicated to wildlife conservation. By collecting our beautifully crafted animal-themed NFTs, you become eligible to participate in our exciting lotteries and support foundations working to protect endangered species.


Our goal is to create a positive impact through art, crypto, and the power of community engagement.

Our NFT Collections

Explore our diverse range of NFT collections dedicated to endangered and other unique animal species. Each NFT represents not only a digital collectible but also our commitment to wildlife conservation.

Bi-Weekly Lottery

Every two weeks, Happy Animals NFT holders participate in an exclusive lottery. If your NFT's collection is selected as the winner, you will share the pot with other collection holders. Additionally, 5% of the pot is donated to wildlife conservation foundations related to the winning animal.

Not only do you stand a chance to win, but your participation directly supports conservation efforts.
